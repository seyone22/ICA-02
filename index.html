Shirakawa-Go

Rooted in History

The Hinamizawa Ryokan is one of the oldest Ryokan in Shirawawa-Go, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. The village is famous for it's 'Gassho Zukuri', or Praying Hands style houses. Experience live as it was in the past, free of all the distractions - but not the comforts - of modern life.

Crusine

Delight in the traditional rural crusine of Shirokawa-Go - Fresh ingredients prepared by locally trained chefs to perfection.
Breakfast and dinner will be provided on request. Please go through the schedule to plan out your perfect trip to Shirakawa-Go.

Amenities

Soak in our refreshing hot-springs, and revitalize yourself before your next adventure in Shirakawa-Go.
For getting around the village, a bicycle rental service is provided. Drop off your luggage in the morning and explore the village before check in!
Please check the schedules for check-in, bathhouse, and onsen use times. Onsen will be availble for private use after 19:00.
