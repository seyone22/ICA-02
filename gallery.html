Experience Rural Countryside Crusine

Paying respect to the time worn traditions of Shirakawa-Go, we've created a fresh experience which combines both new and old - a reawakening of traditional crusine.
Enjoy our hearty and filling set menus, or mix and match from the ala-carte menu.
On weekends, dinner will be prepared over a traditional fire hearth.

Breakfast

Breakfast can be had at the pavillion outside the main Ryokan building, or can be brought up the guest rooms.

Breakfast Time schedule

    07:00 - 09:00

Dinner

Dinner is always a special ocassion. Depending on the season, various local specialties will be on the menu. Please contact us to find out what's on the menu during your stay here!

Dinner Time schedule

    19:00 - 21:00

Enjoy the best of Man and Nature

Shirakawa is a region where man coexists with nature in harmony. Japan is famous for it's four seasons, and Hinamizawa a pictursque view for each one. From vibrant spring to bustling summer - to fiery autumn and cold winter, Hinamizawa is a timeless village with many faces.
